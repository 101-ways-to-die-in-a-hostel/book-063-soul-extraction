# Book Identity

## Core Information

**Book Number:** 063
**Title:** Empty Vessel
**Slug:** soul-extraction
**Category:** Supernatural/Bizarre

## The Death (One Sentence)
A burnout-suffering wellness influencer signs up for a "soul cleansing" ceremony at a Balinese hostel, not realizing the shaman is actually harvesting souls to feed an ancient hunger deity.

## Tagline
"She came to find herself. Something else found her first."

## Themes
1. Commodification of spirituality - satirizes Westerners treating Eastern practices as self-help products
2. The emptiness of "wellness culture" when it lacks genuine substance
3. What actually happens when you "lose yourself" in pursuit of enlightenment

## Tone Balance
- Comedy: 40% (1-100)
- Horror: 40%
- Satire: 20%

## Target Reader
Anyone who's done a yoga retreat to "find themselves," wellness influencers, people who've paid for spiritual experiences without understanding the traditions behind them.

## Comparison Titles
- "Midsommar" meets "Eat Pray Love" with Lovecraftian undertones

## Content Warnings
- Soul/identity destruction
- Cult-like spiritual manipulation
- Body-as-empty-shell horror
- Exploitation of spiritual practices
